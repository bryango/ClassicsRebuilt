Quantum Field Theory program at IAS: Spring Term

The basic weekly schedule for the spring term was: lectures on Thursdays, 10:30-12:30 and 2:00-4:00, problem/discussion sessions on Tuesday and Wednesday afternoons, 1:30-4:00, and on Wednesday evenings beginning at 7:30.

The program concluded with its final lecture on Tuesday, June 10, 1997.
